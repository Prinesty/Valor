About Us

Vision Statement: To be the most celebrated play, movie and stand-up comedy production in Africa and beyond.

Mission Statement: Custodians of Change and Information through Dramatization.

We are Valour Production: we explore popular culture and important social issues in the past, present and future by collaborating with music, dance and acting through the means of new writing. We explore, collaborate and create. We are a new theatre production company who operates on the soul trader basis with joint management by all of the founding members. We aim to produce shows which will utilize new writings, in order to create thetare which will be unique and attractive to our target audience.

Valour's journey started 5 years ago, when a group of like-minded people came together and decided to express their enviable display of dexterity to the world. This has always been the idea at Valour, to connect Ghanaian culture, knowledge and innovation – with the rest of the world. There are so many Productions undertaking the same journey that we started 5 years ago and as such, we invite these businesses and individuals to join the Valour community. A community that goes the extra mile, day in day out to be a change in society through dramatization.
